How to get to Kochi

  • Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK) is the recommended arrival airport.
  • Train: Arrive at Ernakulam Junction or Ernakulam Town and take a taxi to Le Meridien.
  • Road: Kochi is well-connected by highways with cab and self-drive options.
  • Shuttle: A shuttle will be provided for guests from the airport to the hotel.

Things to do in Kochi

  • Tourist sites: Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, Mattancherry Palace, Jew Town, Marine Drive.
  • Food: Seafood in Fort Kochi, Kerala cuisine at local restaurants, and waterfront cafes.
  • Shopping: Spices, handloom, and handicrafts around Jew Town and Lulu Mall.

Visa guidance for international guests

  • Most international guests need a valid visa to enter India. Please confirm your eligibility based on your passport nationality.
  • India e-Visa is available for many travelers. Common categories include e-Tourist, e-Business, and e-Conference visas.
  • Typical requirements include a passport valid for at least 6 months, return/onward travel details, and other supporting information requested during the application.
  • Use the official Government of India portal for latest rules and applications: indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa.
